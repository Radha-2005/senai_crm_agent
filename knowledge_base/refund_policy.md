# Refund Policy

## Overview
SenAI offers transparent and fair refund policies for all subscription tiers.

## Standard Refund Eligibility
- **Trial Period**: Full refund available within **14 days** of first payment, no questions asked.
- **Monthly Subscriptions**: Pro-rated refund for unused days if cancelled within **7 days** of renewal.
- **Annual Subscriptions**: 30-day money-back guarantee from the annual renewal date.

## Eligibility Criteria
Refunds are available when:
1. The customer requests within the eligible window
2. Service outages caused by SenAI exceeded the SLA thresholds
3. The customer was charged incorrectly (billing error)
4. The product materially failed to deliver advertised features

## Non-Refundable Situations
Refunds are NOT available for:
- Requests made after the eligible window
- Partial-month usage beyond the 7-day window
- Custom enterprise contracts (governed by individual DPA/MSA terms)
- Accounts suspended for Terms of Service violations

## Process
1. Customer submits refund request via support@senai.io
2. Support team verifies eligibility within **2 business days**
3. Approved refunds are processed within **5-10 business days** to the original payment method
4. Customer receives email confirmation with refund reference

## Escalated Disputes
If a customer is dissatisfied with the refund decision:
- Escalate to Customer Success Lead within **48 hours**
- For amounts over £1,000: escalate to Finance Director
- Chargeback disputes: respond to bank within **10 business days** with documentation

## Concession Credits
For high-value customers (LTV > £10,000 or Enterprise tier):
- Consider offering service credits instead of cash refunds
- Up to 2 months of service credit may be offered without manager approval
- For larger concessions, Customer Success Director approval required
