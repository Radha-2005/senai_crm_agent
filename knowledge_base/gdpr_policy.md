# GDPR & Data Privacy Policy

## Overview
SenAI is committed to full compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679.
As a data processor and controller, we handle all personal data requests with the highest standards of care.

## Data Subject Rights

### Right of Access (Article 15)
Customers have the right to request access to all personal data held by SenAI.
We will provide a complete export within **30 calendar days** of the verified request.

### Right to Erasure / "Right to Be Forgotten" (Article 17)
Customers may request deletion of their personal data where:
- Data is no longer necessary for the original purpose
- Consent has been withdrawn and no other legal basis exists
- Data has been unlawfully processed

We will complete erasure within **30 calendar days** and provide written confirmation.

### Right to Data Portability (Article 20)
Customers have the right to receive their data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format (JSON or CSV).
We will fulfill portability requests within **30 calendar days**.

### Right to Rectification (Article 16)
Inaccurate personal data will be corrected within **5 business days** of a verified request.

### Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)
Customers may request restriction of processing while accuracy or lawfulness is being contested.

## Data Breach Notification
In the event of a personal data breach, SenAI will:
1. Notify relevant supervisory authorities within **72 hours** of becoming aware
2. Notify affected data subjects without undue delay where high risk exists

## Data Processing Agreements (DPA)
All sub-processors are bound by GDPR-compliant DPAs. Our current sub-processors are listed in our Privacy Notice.
