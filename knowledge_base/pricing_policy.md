# Pricing & Upgrade Policy

## Subscription Plans

### Starter Plan - £49/month or £490/year
- Up to 5 users
- 10,000 emails/month
- Basic analytics
- Email support (48h SLA)

### Professional Plan - £149/month or £1,490/year
- Up to 25 users
- 100,000 emails/month
- Advanced analytics + AI triage
- Priority support (8h SLA)
- API access

### Enterprise Plan - Custom pricing
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited emails
- Custom AI model training
- Dedicated CSM
- 4h SLA support
- Custom SLA terms
- SSO/SAML

## Upgrades and Downgrades

### Mid-Cycle Upgrades
When a customer upgrades mid-billing cycle:
1. Calculate the remaining days in the current period
2. Apply **pro-rata credit** for unused Starter/Professional days
3. Charge the **pro-rata cost** for the new plan for remaining days
4. New billing cycle starts at full new plan price on next renewal date

**Formula**:
```
Pro-rata credit = (days_remaining / total_days) × current_plan_monthly_price
Pro-rata charge = (days_remaining / total_days) × new_plan_monthly_price
Net charge = Pro-rata charge - Pro-rata credit
```

### Seat Add-ons (Enterprise)
- Additional seats beyond plan limit: £15/seat/month
- Pro-rated for mid-cycle additions
- Annual contract: seats billed annually, changes reflected in next renewal

### Downgrade Policy
- Downgrades take effect at end of current billing period
- No refunds for downgrading mid-cycle
- Data retention: 90 days of data accessible after downgrade

## Non-Profit / Educational Discounts
- Registered charities and non-profits: **30% discount** on all plans
- Educational institutions: **40% discount** with valid institution email
- Startups (< 2 years, < 10 employees): **20% discount** for first year

## Annual Plan Discounts
- Pay annually and save up to **20%** vs monthly billing
- Annual plans are non-refundable after 30-day money-back period

## Enterprise Negotiation
For contracts > £10,000/year, the Sales Director can authorize:
- Custom pricing
- Multi-year discount (up to 15% additional)
- Extended payment terms (net-60)
- Custom feature development roadmap priority
